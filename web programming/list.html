KMCT College



KMCT College of Engineering, established in 2001, is one of the most preferred engineering colleges in Kerala. It is affiliated to A.P.J Abdul Kalam Technological University and the University of Calicut. The College offers B.Tech at the Undergraduate level and M.Tech, MBA, MCA at the Postgraduate level. Admissions to the programs are purely based on entrance exams. The college has the right infrastructure and highly qualified faculty team to provide excellent technical education to its students.

About MCA

Master of Computer Application (MCA) is a Two years full-time course postgraduate offered by APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University, Thiruvananthapuram, and approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).

Admission Process

  • Admission is based on an entrance test, a Valid Entrance Score, and a Personal Interview.

Eligiblity Critria

  • Any recognized Bachelor’s Degree of minimum 3 years duration with Mathematics at 10+2 level or at Graduate level with a valid score in the entrance examination being conducted by the Kerala government (LBS Centre for Science and Technology).
  • The candidate should secure a minimum of 50% marks in aggregate for graduation in case of general category, 45% for OBC/SEBC and SC/ST.
